Alfredo Sauce Recipe

This Alfredo sauce recipe always turns out rich and creamy! A delicious way to transform plain fettuccine pasta or steamed vegetables.

Once you try this homemade Alfredo sauce recipe, youâ€™ll never go back to the jarred stuff.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cupButter
  • 1 cupHeavy Cream
  • 1 and 1/2 grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Melt butter in a medium saucepan over low heat. Stir in cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Add cheese and garlic; whisk continuously until heated through. Stir in parsley and serve.
  4. Step 4: Serve immediately and enjoy!
